Restaurant Description
Casurella's is a family-owned, Italian Restaurant. We serve authentic Italian Cuisine in a cozy setting that is perfect for couples and families. In addition, we have spaces for private events. Our dishes are always prepared with the freshest ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible. Aside from the numerous Italian dishes and appetizers, we have a excellent wine collection for discerning tastes.
